NO SLEEP FOR THE WICK KID

"With the price on his head ever increasing, John Wick uncovers a path to defeating The High Table. But before he can earn his freedom, Wick must face off against a new enemy with powerful alliances across the globe and forces that turn old friends into foes."

By Arthur Koton

April 20, 2023

Keanu Reeves stood alone in a toxic desolate wasteland,
surrounded by the remnants of a once-great city. The air was thick with the acrid smell of burning metal, and the ground shook beneath his feet as he looked out at the vast army of machines that lay before him.

He knew he was outnumbered, but he refused to back down. He had seen the devastation that the machines could cause, and he was determined to stop them once and for all. As he prepared for battle, Keanu's mind drifted back to his roles in "The Matrix" and "John Wick." He had played characters who had faced impossible odds before, and he drew strength from their memories.


In "The Matrix," Keanu had played Neo, a hacker who had discovered that his entire world was a simulation created by machines. With the help of a resistance movement, he had fought back against the machines and ultimately defeated their leader, Agent Smith.

In "John Wick," Keanu had played a retired hitman who was forced back into action when a gang of thugs killed his beloved dog. He had taken on the entire criminal underworld, and had emerged victorious through sheer force of will and determination.

Now, as he stood on the brink of another impossible battle, Keanu knew that he could channel the strength and resilience of those characters.

He charged forward, his fists pounding against the metal bodies of the machines. They swarmed around him, their red eyes glowing with malevolent intent, but Keanu refused to give up. He flipped and dodged, evading their attacks with the grace and precision of a martial artist. He fired his guns, taking down the machines one by one, but they just kept coming.

In a moment of inspiration, Keanu remembered a scene from "The Matrix" where Neo had flown through the air, defying the laws of physics. With a fierce determination, Keanu pushed off the ground and soared into the air, his body moving with a fluid grace that seemed almost supernatural.


The machines were caught off guard, and Keanu used the opportunity to strike. He flipped and spun, delivering devastating blows to his metal foes. He fought with a ferocity that surpassed anything he had ever done before, driven by the memory of his beloved dog and the knowledge that he was fighting for the fate of humanity.

In the end, Keanu emerged victorious. The machines lay broken and defeated at his feet, and the world was safe once more.


As he stood in the ruins of the city, Keanu knew that he had channeled the strength and determination of his characters from "The Matrix" and "John Wick."

He had faced impossible odds and emerged victorious, just as they had before him. With a satisfied smile, Keanu walked off into the sunset, ready to face whatever challenges lay ahead.
